GREAT PARENTS ARE MADE - NOT BORN

Ascend.The Aspen Institute

ascend.aspeninstitute.org

Every $1 invested in high-quality early childhood education saves taxpayers at least $7 in social costs later. That investment also increases economic and educational outcomes for children.
High-quality early childhood education is central to closing the achievement gap.
The achievement gap is the difference in academic skills (such as math and literacy) between socioeconomic groups.

Thirty Million Words Initiative, Dr. Dana Suskind

thirtymillionwords.org

A study by researchers Betty Hart and Todd Risley in 1995 found that some children heard thirty million fewer words by their 4th birthdays than others.
The children who heard more words were better prepared when they entered school. These same kids, when followed into third grade, had bigger vocabularies, were stronger readers, and got higher test scores

."Every parent has the words, the language, the nurturance necessary to build their baby's brain."

Zero to Three: Building Connections of a Lifetime

zerotothree.org

A robust body of research has confirmed what parents have always known by instinct: that magic in everyday moments - showing affection, comforting, and playing with children - helps build strong healthy brains.
The first three years of a child’s life are uniquely important because this is the most sensitive period for brain development.
A child’s brain undergoes an amazing period of development from birth to three—producing 700 new neural connections every second.

HOW THRIVE WORKS

Thrive uses Natural Language Processing and Learning Algorithm to deliver you the most personalised tips on parenting.

Natural language processing

Track the amount of words you stimulate your child with daily

Periodic Analytic Reports

Download personalised development report with expert recommendations based on your data

Database of activities

Rely on research and community of parents to use the best educational activities

Friendly reminders

Schedule in time for interactions with your and have the app motivate yout to do it

Family joint account

Add your partner, grandparents, aunties to to track what you can all do better for the baby

Platform for parents and experts

Become a part of a community of users who have expertise and experience with parenting
